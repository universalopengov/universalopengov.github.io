Decentralised Public Institutions

Our mission is to make it extremely easy for any type of public institutions to create decentralised organizations and move their engagements to a public blockchain for transparent auditing by the public. We realise that these organizations need to perform wide array of tasks hence it is necessary that the skeleton of the DAO is kept minimal and the architecture of the DAOs provided is modular. Currently there are a few projects aimed at self sovereign government but they are attempting to create separate governance structure. We believe that even though hierarchical structures are not transparent, they can be highly efficient as they can make separation of concerns possible. Hence, through this project our aim is to make the hierarchical structures transparent so that we can benefit from their efficiency.

Niti Aayog

UOG-Niti-Aayog app on Toshi makes it easier for citizens of India to get involved in the decisions made by Niti Aayog about various government schemes and makes it easier for Niti Aayog to create better decisions that the citizens need.

Right to Information

UOG-RTI app on Toshi makes it easier for citizens of India to track their RTI queries. This also makes the various government departments involved in a query accountable. This makes it possible to create incentive structures for these government organizations
